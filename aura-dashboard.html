How to Use Each Serum

Select a serum to learn exactly how to apply it, who it is for and when to use it.

How to Apply
Application Steps
Cleanse and pat skin dry gently
Dispense 3 to 5 drops onto fingertips
Press gently into face and neck — do not rub
Wait 60 seconds for full absorption
Follow with moisturizer then SPF 30+ (always!)
Who It Is For
Best For
Dull skin, dark spots, hyperpigmentation, all skin types. Start 2 to 3 times per week if you are new to Vitamin C.
✅ Use every morning before SPF for best results.
When to Use
Morning Only ☀️
Always apply in the morning. Vitamin C acts as an antioxidant shield all day — it works best alongside SPF.
⚠️ Never skip SPF — Vitamin C increases UV sensitivity.
Key Warnings
Important Notes
SPF is absolutely mandatory every morning
Do not mix with Benzoyl Peroxide — it deactivates Vitamin C
If serum turns deep brown it has oxidized — replace it
Store in a cool, dark place — use within 3 months of opening
How to Apply
Application Steps
Cleanse and pat dry
Apply 3 to 4 drops as the first serum layer
Press gently into skin — do not tug or drag
Layer other serums or moisturizer on top
Who It Is For
Best For
Oily skin, acne-prone skin, large pores, redness, sensitive skin. The most universally suitable serum in the Aura range.
✅ Ideal first serum for beginners. Visible results in 2 weeks.
When to Use
Morning and Evening ☀️🌙
Twice daily is ideal. Morning use controls shine. Evening use repairs the barrier while you sleep.
✅ Apply before Retinol to reduce irritation — a dermatologist-approved technique.
Key Warnings
Important Notes
Safest active — suitable for almost all skin types
Rarely causes flushing in ultra-sensitive skin — reduce to once daily if needed
Always moisturize — even oily skin needs hydration
How to Apply
Application Steps
Evening only — cleanse thoroughly first
Optional: apply Niacinamide first as a buffer
Apply 2 to 3 drops — avoid direct eye area initially
Wait 20 to 30 minutes before moisturizer
Apply a rich night moisturizer on top
Who It Is For
Best For
Fine lines, aging skin, uneven texture, sun damage. Ages 25 and above.
🤰 Avoid if pregnant or breastfeeding.
When to Use
Evening Only 🌙
Never use in the morning — Retinol degrades in sunlight and increases UV sensitivity significantly.
📅 Start once per week → build to 2 to 3 times per week over 4 to 6 weeks.
Key Warnings
Important Notes
Mandatory SPF every morning during Retinol use
Peeling in the first 4 to 6 weeks is completely normal
Do not use alongside AHAs, BHAs or Vitamin C on the same night
Results: texture in 6 to 8 weeks, fine lines in 3 to 6 months
How to Apply
Application Steps
Cleanse thoroughly — remove all makeup first
Apply 2 to 4 drops on T-zone, chin and nose
Leave on — do not rinse off
Follow with Niacinamide to soothe
Finish with moisturizer — never skip this step
Who It Is For
Best For
Acne-prone skin, oily skin, blackheads, clogged pores, combination skin.
🤰 Avoid during pregnancy — consult your doctor before use.
When to Use
Evening Preferred 🌙
Use 2 to 3 times per week in the evening. If using in the morning, SPF is mandatory.
📅 Alternate with Retinol — never use both on the same evening.
Key Warnings
Important Notes
Never use daily — 2 to 3 times per week maximum
Initial purging in the first 4 weeks is normal — the serum is working
Always use SPF the morning after use
Pairs beautifully with Niacinamide for a soothing effect

What Works Together

Smart combinations unlock results. Knowing what to avoid prevents setbacks.

Safe to Layer Together
Niacinamide + Salicylic Acid Vitamin C + Niacinamide Retinol + Niacinamide Salicylic + Retinol (alternate nights)
Use With Caution
Vitamin C + AHA / BHA (same time) Retinol + Direct acids Multiple actives at once (beginners)
Avoid Together
Retinol + Vitamin C (same session) Salicylic + Retinol (same night) Vitamin C + Benzoyl Peroxide
